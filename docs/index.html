Privacy Policy

Jmath ("App", "we") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use our app and how that information is used. By downloading and using our app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information Collected by Us

The "Jmath" app does not collect or store any personal data (name, email, location, etc.) directly from users. Except for the essential functions required for the app to work, we do not access or process your personal information.

2. Third-Party Data Collection: Unity Ads

To cover the development and maintenance costs of our app, we use a third-party advertising service, Unity Ads.

Information Collected by Unity Ads:

  • Device Information: Technical details like operating system and device model.
  • Advertising ID (AD_ID): A resettable identifier used for personalized advertising, such as Google Advertising ID.
  • Network Information: Information necessary to monitor internet connection and network status.
  • Ad Interaction Data: Anonymous data regarding the ads you see and how you interact with them.

Purpose of Data Use:

This data is used by Unity Ads to display ads, measure the effectiveness of ad campaigns, and prevent fraud. Unity is responsible for the data it collects and processes it according to its own privacy policies. For more information, please review Unity's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Sharing

Since our app does not collect data directly, there is no personal data shared by us with third parties. Data collection and sharing are performed only by our advertising partner Unity according to their policies.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app does not target children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them. Unity Ads has mechanisms to serve age-appropriate ads. If you believe your child has inadvertently shared data, please review Unity's data management options.

5. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated Date." It is important to review the policy regularly.
